# Equipment Comparison Study

Evaluated 5 commercial electric kettles for the tea shop upgrade. Winner: Bonavita 1.7L variable temperature. Criteria: temperature accuracy (+/- 1C), build quality, warranty, capacity. Runner-up: Fellow Stagg EKG (better design, lower capacity). Both models support the four temperature presets needed for our tea menu. Decision: Bonavita for back-of-house, Fellow for customer-facing counter.
