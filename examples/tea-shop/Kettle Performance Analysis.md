# Kettle Performance Analysis

Comparison of current stovetop kettle vs electric alternatives. Stovetop: 6 min to boil 1.5L, no temperature control, requires staff attention. Electric (proposed): 3.5 min to boil, programmable presets, auto-shutoff. ROI calculation: electric kettle saves 45 min/day staff time at current volume. Payback period: 3 weeks at GBP 89 unit cost.
