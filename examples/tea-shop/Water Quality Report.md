# Water Quality Report

Municipal water tested monthly via third-party lab. Key metrics: hardness 180mg/L CaCO3 (moderate), chlorine 0.3mg/L, pH 7.2. Installed carbon block filter in 2025-06 to reduce chlorine taste. Post-filter chlorine: <0.05mg/L. Blind taste tests showed 85% of staff preferred filtered water for brewing. Filter replacement schedule: every 3 months.
