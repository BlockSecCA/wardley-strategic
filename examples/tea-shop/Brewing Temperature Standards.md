# Brewing Temperature Standards

Internal quality manual for water temperature by tea type. Black tea: 96C, steep 3-5 min. Green tea: 75-80C, steep 2-3 min. White tea: 70-75C, steep 4-5 min. Herbal: 100C, steep 5-7 min. Electric kettle presets calibrated quarterly against reference thermometer. Last calibration: 2026-02-15, all readings within 2C tolerance.
